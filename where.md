# Where am I?

Here's where you can find me on the internet -- in rough order of most active to least active.

- [GitHub](https://github.com/pepopowitz): I am in GitHub many many times every day.
- [Bluesky](): This is my main. Occasional posts about tech, disc golf, art stuff, etc. I check this daily.
- [Threads](https://www.threads.net/@pepopowitz): My Bsky feed isn't active enough and when my brain wants more scrolling, I absorb stuff from Threads. I usually check this daily.
- [YouTube](www.youtube.com/@FriendsWithCode): FriendsWithCode, a programming stream I did with some friends! It's currently inactive, but it's still a good way to get a sense of how I like to work on code.
- [LinkedIn](https://linkedin.com/in/stevenjhicks/): All tech/job posts. For a short period of time I thought LinkedIn was actually the most interesting tech content...now it's all ai;dr and I avoid it. I probably won't accept a connection request unless I've met you in person.
- [Podcast](https://anchor.fm/a-developer-experience): My podcast about being a human in tech -- A Developer Experience Podcast -- is dormant. The old episodes are still pretty good and you should listen to them.
- [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/pepopowitz/): Mostly non-tech posts.
- [Email](mailto:steven.j.hicks@gmail.com): Yes, I'll definitely get your email. Will I respond? 🤷 I wish I could say that I was not the kind of person who forgets to respond to an email until it becomes too uncomfortable to respond.....but I am that person.
- [Mastodon](https://hachyderm.io/@pepopowitz): This used to be my main, but I don't really like the idea of infinite walled gardens. I'm basically dormant here, unless you engage me directly.
- [Dev.to](https://dev.to/pepopowitz): Syndicated blog posts from here, but not all of them.
